Restaurant & Bar

Saddle up and head to our Restaurant Upstairs, where the wild west meets modern comfort. Here, you'll find an authentic cowboy bar that serves up ice-cold drinks, perfect for quenching your thirst after a long day on the trail. With big screens strategically placed throughout, you won’t miss a single moment of your favorite sports action while you enjoy a hearty meal. Whether you're here to share tall tales, watch the big game, or just soak up the western vibe, our restaurant offers the perfect blend of rustic charm and lively entertainment.

Filling Station

We don’t just sell fuel; we offer a pit stop where you can catch your breath, dust off your boots, and get ready for the road ahead. Fill your tank, tip your hat, and get back to blazing trails.

Service Center

When your trusty steed needs a little TLC, there’s no better place than our Service Center. Our seasoned mechanics know how to keep everything from wagons to modern machines running smooth. In a state-of-the-art facility with a western twist, we treat your ride with the care it deserves, ensuring it’s ready for whatever the frontier throws your way. About Us

The Dusty Saddle isn’t just a place—it’s a living, breathing slice of the wild west. Born from a passion for cowboy culture and a love for the open road, we’ve created a destination where modern convenience meets old-world charm. From the wooden beams overhead to the cowboy boots on our feet, every inch of this place is steeped in tradition. So whether you’re a drifter passing through or a local looking for a taste of the frontier, we invite you to saddle up and experience the legend of the old west, right here at
The Dusty Saddle.
